About Evo Solar

Locally owned and operated in Victoria — creating tailored, sustainable energy solutions since 2010.

The Evo Solar Difference

With a passion for all things renewable energy, business founder Cam Pike started Evo Solar to protect the future of the industry, as well as the future of his three young children.

In his initial role as an inspector of solar system installations across Victoria, Cam recognised the key issues that solar customers were facing. As director of Evo Solar, his goal is to remove the confusion around solar power and product selection by providing customers with quality service and advice.

Now one of the most recognised Victorian solar companies, Cam and his team are proud to help customers install renewable energy systems that not only fit their needs and budget but also provide a sustainable, greener future.

  • CEC & SAA accredited installers
  • New Energy Tech Approved Seller
  • Certified Enphase Gold installer
  • Licensed electrician inspectors
  • 3,000+ systems installed across Victoria

Quality You Can Trust

Striving to be the best solar provider across Victoria, Evo Solar is also recognised as a New Energy Tech Approved Seller. With CEC, SAA solar and battery accreditations, the Evo Solar team are licensed electrician inspectors with the knowledge and experience to create sustainable, energy efficient homes.

We use only equipment and products that we know and trust. Our range of solar panels, inverters, batteries, heat pumps, EV chargers and more provide quality solar power and green energy solutions to homes across Victoria.
