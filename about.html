About Evo Solar

Geelong and the Surf Coast's trusted solar specialists, proudly servicing homes across Victoria.

Our Story

Welcome to Evo Solar

At Evo Solar, we design and install high-quality home energy solutions built around your home, your energy needs and your long-term goals.

With more than 15 years of industry experience, our team focuses on practical, reliable green energy solutions designed to perform in the real world, helping you save money, use more of your own power and become less reliant on the grid.

Evo Solar was founded by Cameron Pike, a licensed A-grade electrician, SAA-accredited solar and battery installer, and experienced electrical inspector. Cam's technical background plays a big part in how we do things. We listen, take the time to understand your needs, recommend equipment we trust and design systems correctly from the start.

Why Choose Us

Your Long-Term Energy Partner

We are not here to just sell you a system and disappear. Our goal is to be your long-term energy partner, helping you reduce your bills, improve your energy independence, add backup power where needed, and move towards a cleaner, more sustainable home.

  • 15+ years of solar, battery and electrical experience
  • Over 3,000 solar systems installed across Victoria
  • CEC and SAA-accredited solar and battery installers
  • Licensed electricians and experienced installation teams
  • High-quality equipment from proven, well-supported manufacturers
  • Honest advice focused on performance, reliability and results
  • Local support from a team based in Geelong and the Surf Coast
  • 25-year workmanship warranty on all installations
Evo Care

Support That Continues After Installation

Our service does not end once your system is installed. Evo Care is our commitment to supporting you for the life of your system. From workmanship and compliance through to monitoring, performance checks and ongoing advice, our team is here to make sure your solar and battery system keeps doing what it was designed to do.

When you choose Evo Solar, you are choosing a local team that stands behind its work.
