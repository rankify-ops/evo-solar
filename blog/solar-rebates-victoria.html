What Solar Rebate Systems Are Available in Victoria?

Published June 28, 2023

Victoria is one of the best places in Australia to go solar, thanks to a combination of federal and state government incentive programs. These rebates and financial support schemes can significantly reduce the upfront cost of installing solar panels and batteries, making clean energy accessible to more households than ever.

Federal Government: Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs)

Under the federal Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme (SRES), homeowners who install an eligible solar panel system receive Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs). The number of STCs you receive depends on your system size, location, and the remaining years until the scheme phases down in 2030.

In practice, your solar installer typically handles the STC process and applies the discount directly to your quote, so you see the saving upfront without any paperwork. For a standard residential system, this can represent a discount of several thousand dollars off the total cost.

Victorian Government: Solar Homes Program — Panel Rebate

The Victorian Government's Solar Homes Program offers eligible homeowners a rebate of up to $1,400 towards the cost of a solar panel system. In addition, you may be eligible for an interest-free loan of up to $1,400 to cover the remaining cost, making it even more affordable to get started with solar.

To qualify, your home must be valued under a certain threshold, you must be an owner-occupier, and the property must not already have a solar system installed. The rebate is applied at the point of sale by your approved installer, so you only pay the reduced amount.

Solar Battery Rebate and Loan — No Longer Available

The Solar Victoria battery rebate and interest-free loan program has now closed. However, federal STCs now apply to battery installations, saving you up to $6,600 upfront. Adding a battery to your solar system maximises your self-consumption and can provide backup power during outages.

Rebates for Rental Properties

The Solar Homes Program also extends to rental properties. Landlords of eligible rental homes can access a rebate towards the cost of installing solar panels, which benefits both the property owner and the tenant through lower electricity costs. This is part of Victoria's broader push to ensure that the benefits of solar energy are available to renters as well as homeowners.
