FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about solar batteries answered by our experts.

How much does a solar battery cost?

The cost of a solar battery depends on the brand, capacity, and installation requirements. Prices typically range from $8,000 to $16,000 for a standard residential system. Government rebates can significantly reduce this cost. Contact us for a personalised quote based on your energy needs and eligibility for rebates.

Can I add a battery to my existing solar system?

Yes, in most cases a battery can be added to an existing solar system. The compatibility depends on your current inverter type. Some batteries like the Tesla Powerwall have their own built-in inverter, making them compatible with almost any solar setup. Our team will assess your current system and recommend the best battery solution for your situation.

Is there a government rebate on solar batteries?

Yes, the Victorian Government offers battery rebates for eligible households through the Solar Homes Program. Combined with the federal Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme (SRES), you could save thousands on your battery installation. Contact us to check your eligibility and find out how much you could save.
