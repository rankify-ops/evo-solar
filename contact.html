FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about getting in touch and working with Evo Solar.

How do I get a quote?

Getting a quote is easy and obligation-free. Simply fill out the form above with your details and we'll be in touch within 24 hours. Alternatively, give us a call on 0411 375 484 and we'll walk you through your options over the phone. We'll arrange a time to assess your property and provide a tailored quote based on your energy needs.

What areas do you service?

We proudly service all of Victoria, including Melbourne, Geelong, the Surf Coast, and surrounding areas. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, get in touch and we'll let you know.

How long does installation take?

Usually 1-2 days depending on system size. A standard residential solar installation is typically completed in a single day, while larger systems or those with batteries may take a second day. We'll confirm the timeline when we provide your quote.

Do you offer maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and system health checks to ensure your solar system is performing at its best. Regular maintenance helps maximise your energy output and extends the life of your system. Contact us to book a maintenance visit.
