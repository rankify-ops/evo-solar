Understanding Solar Rebates in Victoria

Multiple government programs are available to reduce the upfront cost of going solar.

Federal Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs)

STCs are a federal government incentive that provides a point-of-sale discount on your solar system. The number of certificates you receive depends on the size of your system, your location, and the amount of renewable energy it will produce over its lifetime. Evo Solar applies this discount directly to your quote, so you see the savings immediately — no waiting for reimbursements.

Victorian Solar Homes Program

The Victorian Government’s Solar Homes Program offers rebates to eligible homeowners looking to install solar panels. This rebate can significantly reduce the upfront cost of your solar system. Eligibility criteria apply, including property value thresholds and ownership requirements. Evo Solar will check your eligibility and handle the entire application process on your behalf.

Solar Battery Loans

The Victorian Government offers interest-free loans to help homeowners add battery storage to their solar systems. Batteries let you store excess solar energy for use at night or during peak times, further reducing your electricity bills. Combined with a solar battery rebate, this makes energy storage more affordable than ever.

Feed-in Tariffs

Feed-in tariffs allow you to get paid for excess solar energy that you export back to the electricity grid. The rate varies by retailer, so it’s worth comparing offers. Evo Solar can advise on the best feed-in tariff options available in your area to help you maximise your return on investment.

FAQ

Rebate Questions Answered

Common questions about solar rebates and incentives.

Am I eligible for solar rebates?

Eligibility depends on several factors. For the Victorian Solar Homes Program, you generally need to be a Victorian homeowner with a combined household income under the threshold and a property value under the specified limit. For federal STCs, most Australian homeowners installing an eligible solar system qualify. We’ll check your eligibility during your free consultation.

How much can I save?

Savings vary depending on the size of your system, your location, and which rebates you’re eligible for. Combined federal STCs and state rebates can save you thousands of dollars on your solar installation. We calculate your exact savings as part of your personalised quote.

Do I need to apply separately?

No — we handle everything for you. Evo Solar manages all rebate applications, STC registrations, and paperwork as part of our service. You don’t need to deal with any government departments or fill in any forms yourself.

Are there battery rebates too?

Yes — the Victorian Government offers interest-free loans for solar battery installations. Combined with available rebates, this makes adding battery storage to your solar system very affordable. We can include battery options in your quote and handle all the loan paperwork.
