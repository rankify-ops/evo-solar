Rebates & Incentives

Take advantage of government rebates and incentives to make your solar investment even more affordable. Evo Solar will guide you through the process.

Solar Panels

Victorian Solar Panel Rebate

The Victorian Government offers rebates of up to $1,400 on solar panel systems for eligible households. This rebate, combined with Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs) from the federal government, can significantly reduce the upfront cost of going solar.

  • Up to $1,400 rebate on solar panel systems
  • Available for owner-occupiers with a combined household income under $210,000
  • Property value must be under $3 million
  • Must use a CEC-accredited installer like Evo Solar
Batteries

Solar Battery Rebate — No Longer Available

While the Solar Victoria battery rebate has closed, federal STCs now apply to battery installations — saving you up to $6,600 upfront. Pairing solar panels with a battery maximises your energy independence and can significantly reduce your electricity bills.

  • Federal STCs for batteries — up to $6,600 off
  • Store excess solar energy for night-time use
  • Reduce reliance on the grid
  • Maximise your return on solar investment
Heat Pumps

Hot Water System Rebate

The Victorian Government provides rebates on heat pump hot water systems, making it more affordable to switch from gas or electric storage systems to an energy-efficient heat pump.

  • Rebate available for heat pump hot water systems
  • Replace old gas or electric hot water systems
  • Save up to 80% on hot water energy costs
  • Reduce your household carbon emissions
Federal Incentive

Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs)

The federal government's Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme (SRES) provides financial incentives through STCs. These certificates are created when you install eligible solar panels, batteries, or heat pumps and are applied as an upfront discount on your installation cost.

  • Federal incentive applied as upfront discount
  • Now available for batteries — up to $6,600 off
  • Automatically factored into your Evo Solar quote
  • Available for solar panels, batteries and heat pumps

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about rebates and incentives answered by our experts.

Am I eligible for the Victorian solar rebate?

To be eligible, you must be an owner-occupier, have a combined household income under $210,000, and your property value must be under $3 million. The system must be installed by a CEC-accredited installer like Evo Solar.

Can I combine multiple rebates?

Yes, you can combine the Victorian solar panel rebate with federal STCs. Batteries now also qualify for STCs (up to $6,600), and heat pump installations attract their own STCs too. We'll show you the full breakdown in your quote.

How much can I save with rebates?

Savings vary depending on the system you install and your eligibility. With combined state and federal incentives, you could save thousands on your solar installation. Contact Evo Solar for a detailed quote showing your potential savings.
