FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about split system air conditioners answered by our experts.

What are the benefits of a split system air conditioner?

Split system air conditioners offer improved energy efficiency, quieter operation, and better cooling or heating capacity. They also allow for more flexibility in placement, as the outdoor unit can be placed away from the indoor unit.

How do I know what size split system to buy?

Choosing the right size air conditioner is important as it affects performance and energy use. Evo Solar will work with you to determine the right unit for your needs.

What's the difference between an inverter and non-inverter split system?

An inverter split system uses a variable speed compressor which adjusts to the needs of the system, providing more precise temperature control and better energy efficiency. A non-inverter operates at a fixed speed, turning off and on to maintain temperature, which can result in greater energy consumption.
