FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about solar panels answered by our experts.

What should I look for when choosing solar panels?

When choosing solar panels, look for quality materials and build, a low temperature coefficient (which affects performance in hot weather), a comprehensive warranty from a reputable manufacturer, and a strong brand reputation. Efficiency ratings, durability certifications, and the track record of the manufacturer are all key indicators of a quality panel.

Why should I choose high-quality solar panels?

High-quality solar panels deliver better performance over a longer lifespan. They are built to withstand the elements including extreme heat, hail, and strong winds. Premium panels also come with extended warranties (25-40 years), giving you peace of mind and better long-term return on your investment.

Is there a government rebate on solar panels?

Yes, the Victorian Government offers rebates on solar panel installations for eligible households. The federal Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme (SRES) also provides financial incentives through Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs). Visit solar.vic.gov.au for the latest information on available rebates and eligibility criteria.
