FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about EV chargers answered by our experts.

What type of electric vehicle is compatible with EV chargers?

Most plug-in vehicles will be compatible with an EV charger, but it's important to check the specifications of your car before purchasing a charger. Some cars have built-in charging ports that are only compatible with certain types of chargers. Talk to Evo Solar today to discuss the best EV charger solution for your home.

Can I install an EV charger in my garage or outside?

Yes, most EV chargers can be installed either indoors or outdoors. If you choose to install the charger outside, we will ensure that it is in an area that is protected from the weather. Evo Solar will also make sure that any electrical wiring is of a suitable type and meets the necessary safety standards.

Is it possible to install multiple chargers at home?

Yes, it's possible to install multiple charging points in order to meet the needs of multiple electric vehicles. If you have more than one car, we will ensure that your electrical system can handle the load.
