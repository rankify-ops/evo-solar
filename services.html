FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our solar services.

How long does installation take?

Most residential solar installations are completed in 1–2 days, depending on the system size and complexity. Larger commercial systems may take longer. We’ll provide a clear timeline during your consultation.

Do you handle all the paperwork?

Yes — we take care of everything. From Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs) and government rebate applications to grid connection paperwork, our team handles all the administration so you don’t have to.

What warranty do you offer?

We offer a minimum 25-year panel warranty from the manufacturer, plus our own workmanship guarantee on all installations. Inverter warranties vary by brand but typically range from 10–25 years.

How often should I maintain my system?

We recommend an annual health check to keep your system running at peak performance. This includes panel cleaning, connection inspections, and a full performance review. Regular maintenance helps catch small issues before they become costly problems.
