FAQS

How long will it take for my solar power system to be installed?

​​Installation usually takes a few days to complete, depending on the size of the system. Our in-house, CEC-accredited installers will mount the solar panels on your roof and connect them to the inverter. This converts the DC electricity generated by the panels into AC electricity that can be used in your home. Once installation is complete, Evo Solar will commission the system and connect it to the grid.

How long will it take for my solar system to pay for itself?

This will depend on your household’s usage, as well as the cost of your system. Generally, you can expect your system to pay for itself within 3-6 years.

Can I install a battery later?

Yes, you can install a solar battery at a later time, however installing a solar battery can be easier and more cost-effective if it’s done in conjunction with an existing solar installation. If you’re already set up with solar, Evo Solar can provide advice and installation on the right solar battery for your home.
