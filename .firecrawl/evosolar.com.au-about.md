## What makes Evo Solar the right choice?

When it comes to solar companies in Geelong and the Surf Coast, you can trust Evo Solar.

![](https://evosolar.com.au/app/uploads/2023/07/CamAboutImage-scaled.jpg)

# THE EVO SOLAR DIFFERENCE

With a passion for all things renewable energy, business founder Cam Pike started Evo Solar to protect the future of the industry, as well as the future of his three young children.

In his initial role as an inspector of solar system installations across Victoria, Cam recognised the key issues that solar customers were facing. As director of Evo Solar, his goal is to remove the confusion around solar power and product selection by providing customers with quality service and advice.

Now one of the most recognised Geelong solar companies, Cam and his team are proud to help customers install renewable energy systems that not only fit their needs and budget but also provide a sustainable, greener future.

Striving to be the best solar provider across Geelong and Melbourne, Evo Solar is also recognised as a New Energy Tech Approved Seller.

With CEC, SAA Soalr and battery accreditations, the Evo Solar team are licenced electrician inspectors with the knowledge and experience to create sustainable, energy efficient homes.
