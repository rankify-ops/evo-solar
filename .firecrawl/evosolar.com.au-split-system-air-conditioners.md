FAQS

What are the benefits of a split system air conditioner?

Split system air conditioners offer a range of advantages, including improved energy efficiency and quieter operation. They also provide better cooling or heating capacity than other types of air conditioning system. They also allow for more flexibility in placement, as the outdoor unit can be placed away from the indoor unit.

How do I know what size split system air conditioner to buy?

Choosing the right size air conditioner for your home is important as it affects how well your unit will perform and how much energy it will use. Evo Solar will work with you to determine the right unit for your needs.

What's the difference between an inverter and non-inverter split system air conditioner?

An inverter split system air conditioner uses a variable speed compressor which adjusts to the needs of the system, providing more precise temperature control and better energy efficiency. A non-inverter split system operates at a fixed speed, turning off and on to maintain the desired temperature, which can result in greater energy consumption.
