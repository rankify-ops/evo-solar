FAQS

What should I look for when choosing the right solar panels?

To ensure the best quality solar panels for your home, consider these key factors:

- Quality materials for durability and efficiency: choose high-quality panels that can withstand harsh weather conditions and have a longer lifespan.
- Temperature coefficient: Look for panels with low temperature coefficients, as they perform better in hot weather.
- Warranty: opt for panels with warranties of at least 15 years.
- Brand reputation: select panels from reputable brands known for producing high-quality solar solutions.
- Performance guarantee: some manufacturers offer performance guarantees for a specified period of time.
- Sustainable manufacturing: high-quality panels use sustainable manufacturing techniques, including renewable energy, recycling bi-products and ethical sourcing of materials.

Why should I choose high-quality solar panels?

Solar is an investment, so you’ll want to ensure you get the most value from your purchase. Although they generally cost more, high-quality solar panels will perform better and are able to withstand the elements better than cheaper panels. They’re often manufactured using sustainable renewable methods — an important consideration if sustainability is one of your reasons for switching to a renewable energy system. Reputable manufacturers typically provide extended warranty periods as a testament to their quality craftsmanship.
