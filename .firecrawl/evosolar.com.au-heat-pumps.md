FAQS

How do heat pump hot water systems work?

Heat Pump Hot Water System uses leading technology to draw energy in from the ambient atmosphere and transfer it into heat. Ozone friendly refrigerant (CO2) absorbs the heat, which then flows into a compressor, where it’s converted into a high temperature gas. It then passes through a condenser within the water heat exchanger and the water is heated and then stored in the hot water tank for later use. No backup booster is required.

How long do heat pump hot water systems last?

Heat pumps have a lifespan of around 10-15 years, depending on how well you maintain them and how often they are used. If you keep up with regular maintenance and use the system correctly, it should last for many years.
